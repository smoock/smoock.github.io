Recie Privacy Policy. Recie does not collect, store or share any personal information about you. We securely store the email address and password of your Recie account and store a copy of your recipes as well as photos on our servers in order to sync the data to your other devices. Recie uses your internet connection when syncing your data and when you import recipes from web sites. We will retain your data for as long as you maintain an account or as otherwise necessary to provide you with access to our services. We will also retain your data as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
